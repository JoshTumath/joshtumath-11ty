It's always a privilege to speak at conferences; a privilege that I don't take lightly. The people who gave their time to helping others through blogs and conference talks are a big part of why I wanted to get involved in the open web and web standards, so it's wonderful to be able to give back in that way.

This page is a record of the conferences and meetups that I have spoken at over the years.
